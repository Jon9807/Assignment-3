Meet Our Instructors

Terence Fletcher – Jazz Percussion Instructor

Bio: A tough but passionate mentor, Fletcher pushes students beyond their limits to unlock their full potential.

Background: Master jazz drummer, renowned for his rigorous teaching methods.

Instruments: Drums, Percussion.

Philosophy: “There’s no two words in the English language more harmful than ‘good job.'”

Tom – Guitar and Composition

Bio: Tom started as a street performer, blending classical techniques with modern rock. His unique style caught the attention of major labels.

Background: Studied under jazz legends, later revolutionizing guitar theory with his minimalist approach.

Instruments: Guitar, Bass, Mandolin.

Philosophy: “You can never play too many notes… unless you do.”

Severus Snape – Classical Music Theory

Bio: A strict but respected figure in music theory, feared by students who dare misplace a leading tone.

Background: Once a child prodigy, Snape mastered counterpoint, fugue writing, and orchestration before the age of 15.

Instruments: Piano, Organ, Harpsichord.

Philosophy: “If you break the rules of harmony, at least do it intelligently.”

Johann Sebastian Bach – Baroque Master

Bio: A genius of counterpoint, Bach wrote music so precise it could be used in physics equations.

Background: Created fugues so complex even computers struggle to analyze them.

Instruments: Organ, Harpsichord, Violin.

Philosophy: “It’s easy to play any instrument: all you have to do is touch the right key at the right time.”

IU (Lee Ji-eun) – K-Pop & Acoustic Songwriting

Bio: A self-made artist who went from teenage singer-songwriter to one of South Korea’s most beloved and versatile musicians.

Background: Blends soft ballads, jazzy vocals, and pop melodies while writing deeply personal lyrics that connect with millions.

Instruments: Voice, Guitar, Piano.

Philosophy: “Music should feel like a letter to yourself—whether it’s a love song, a story, or a dream you’re chasing.”

Lana Del Rey – Alternative & Vintage Pop

Bio: The queen of melancholic storytelling, Lana Del Rey’s music blends vintage Hollywood glamour with modern alternative pop.

Background: Started as an underground artist before redefining pop music with cinematic, nostalgic ballads.

Instruments: Vocals, Piano, Guitar.

Philosophy: “If it sounds like a sad poem and makes you feel like you’re in an old movie, you’re doing it right.”
